Digital Design with a focus on systems and accessibility

I am a Digital Designer (Cand.IT) based in Aarhus, Denmark, with a passion for creating structured, scalable, and inclusive digital experiences. For the past three years, I have been working within the healthcare sector, transforming complex legacy systems into modern, user-centric web solutions. My expertise lies in the technical execution of UI design. I thrive when building robust design systems in Figma and ensuring that every component is not only visually polished but also fully accessible and WCAG 2.2 compliant.
